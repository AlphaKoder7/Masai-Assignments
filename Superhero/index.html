Bio

Thor is the mighty God of Thunder, hailing from Asgard. He is known for wielding Mjolnir, a powerful enchanted hammer that grants him the ability to control lightning and harness immense strength. Thor is a founding member of the Avengers and has defended Earth from numerous threats.

Skills

  • Masterful wielder of Mjolnir
  • Control over lightning and storms
  • Superhuman strength and endurance
  • Skilled warrior and strategist
  • Immortal and long-lived
